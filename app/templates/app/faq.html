FAQ

How do I begin requesting or joining rides?

If you are a Princeton student, begin by simply logging in with your netid. Then, choose the category of your ride to view currently open requests. If you see one you like, request to join. If not, click on "Create New Request" to make your own.

Will you be offering this service outside of Princeton?

Going into this project, we wanted to grant Princeton students the convenience and comfort of sharing rides with other Princeton students. As our app takes off at Princeton, we definitely hope to offer local versions for fans of our service elsewhere!

How can I get involved with this project?

Contact any one of the team at our @princeton.edu email, and we'll get back to you soon!
